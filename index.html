Tenuta Guado al Tasso is located in the small and prestigious Bolgheri DOC, on the coast of the Alta Maremma, about a hundred kilometers southwest of Florence. This denomination has a relatively short history (born in 1995) but boasts international fame as a new reference point in the global wine scene.

Tenuta Tignanello is located in the heart of the Chianti Classico, on the gentle hills enclosed between the valleys of the Greve and the Pesa, and covers 319 hectares of land, with about 130 hectares cultivated. Among its vineyards, two treasures stand out: the Tignanello vineyard and the Solaia vineyard, which extend over the same hill, on soils derived from marine marls of the Pliocene with limestone and shale, and enjoy hot days and cool nights during the growing season.

Vigna dell'alto Mare is an important milestone: this name has, in fact, almost a hundred years of history. At the same time, it represents a continuous evolution. One fixed point: the search for ever higher quality margins. This idea has given rise to two different wines.
